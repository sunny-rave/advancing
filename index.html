★Load In★
We require at least 1.5 hours before doors to load in decor, equipment, and merch, plus soundcheck and visual testing. We travel with a rental car — please let us know where to park. Load-out requires at least 45 minutes unless the venue can assist.
★Run of Show★
Unless otherwise discussed, we handle all programming for the duration of the show. Typical order: METTER opens → dj pressed headlines → dj pressed b2b w/ Greenman → Greenman closes.

★Art Gallery★
One of the most beloved parts of Sunny Rave is the art gallery. We need a dedicated space and/or wall to display merch, physical props, and artwork. We may ship decor ahead of time — usually just a few Amazon packages. High-top bar tables are ideal. We can also use wall-safe adhesive. See examples here →
★Cocktail Menu★
In the spirit of bringing Paddy's Pub to life, we work with each venue to create custom cocktails. The bar finalizes and displays the exact menu.

Example Drinks

🍷 Canned Wine (reference)

Exactly what it sounds like. Examples: Bev Rosé, Underwood, Champagne

🥛 Fight Milk (reference)

Any white drink or slushie. Examples: Frozen Margarita, Piña Colada, White Russian

🔵 Riot Juice (reference)

Any blue-colored drink. Example: Blue Curaçao + Lemon-Lime Soda

🫧 Blue Hole (reference)

Any blue drink — must be served in an extra large container with multiple straws. Example: Adios MF

🥃 Wolf Cola (reference)

Any well drink with cola. Example: Rum & Coke
